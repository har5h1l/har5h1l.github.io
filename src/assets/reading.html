My 2025 Reading Journey

After a long rut, I've finally gotten inspired to get back into reading outside of what was required in school — and it's been one of the best decisions I've made this year. Inspired by creators like John Fish and his deep-dive reading goals, I set out to build a consistent routine where reading is how I start my day.

To make this happen, I had to reshape my daily rhythm. Before, my sleep schedule was totally out of control — I'd stay up way too late, and it never felt urgent to sleep "on time." Now, I'm waking up earlier and going to sleep earlier too, giving my mornings more clarity and purpose. Instead of cramming everything at night, I split up my work — it's made me more productive and more mindful.

Reading has become a quiet but powerful start to my day. With consistent rest, I've noticed my focus and creativity return, and I'm taking away so much from the books I read.

This page is where I'm documenting my reading journey — what I've read, what I'm reading, and what's on the horizon. If you've also been meaning to read more, come join me.
